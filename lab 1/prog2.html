Rajama

About Rajma (Kidney Beans)

Rajma is a popular Indian legume made from kidney-shaped beans. It's widely used in Indian households, especially in North India, as a part of everyday meals. Rajma is usually cooked with spices, onions, tomatoes, and garlic, and served with rice – a famous combo called "Rajma Chawal."

About Dosa

Dosa is a thin, crispy, and savory South Indian pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram lentils). It’s typically served with sambar (a lentil soup) and coconut chutney.
